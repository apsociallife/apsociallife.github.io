Hello from 134th Street in the East Renton Highlands, Washington. Every Christmas we transform our front yard into a walk-through Christmas light experience. 2025 was our 3rd year and we had 112,014 lights... not that anyone is counting! Our experience is a little differant though. We invite everyone to park in our large driveway and walk through the lights on the many little pathways we've created.

We try to create a special place to spend time with friends and family, get some great Christmasy photos, and be dazzled by lights lights lights everywhere. It will always be completely free, it's kid and even dog friendly, and it's open whether we're home or not.

We hope to see you when we reopen the day after Thanksgiving 2026!
